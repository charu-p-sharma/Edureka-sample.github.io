About Us

At Edureka Learning Center, we believe in imparting the latest, in-demand, tech skills through our exclusively curated courses to bridge the gap between industry demand and talent supply.

Our mission is to provide you with an excellent learning experience and help you attract the jobs of your dreams. At Edureka Learning Center, our students are not mere learners, they are the future IT professionals.

SOME COMMON QUERIES

Edureka Learning Center is powered by Edureka, the industry leader in the online professional and higher education sector in the emerging tech and management domains. Founded in 2011, Edureka pioneered Instructor-led live online training in India under the supervision of professional instructors, all under the umbrella motive of helping students upskill.
In September 2021, Veranda, a Public Listed entity, acquired Edureka as part of its mission to build a full-stack education business catering to different strata of learners encompassing virtually every level of Indian society.

Edureka's founder is Lovleen Bhatia.

Edureka is an e-learning platform providing live, online training in trending technologies. It provides comprehensive courses in Big Data & Analytics, Cloud Computing, Programming, Application Development, and other trending technologies. The company was founded in 2011 and is based in Bangalore, India.

How much does an Edureka course cost? The cost of Edureka's courses varies from 4610 to 76,835 rupees, with an average expense of about 36,880 rupees.

Edureka students have been placed in many businesses with a global presence through the Career and Placement Assistance Programs.
